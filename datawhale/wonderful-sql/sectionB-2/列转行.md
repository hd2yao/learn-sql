### 练习二：列转行
假设 A B C 三位小朋友期末考试成绩如下所示：
```plain
+-----+-----------+------|---------|
| name|   chinese | math | english |
+-----+-----------+------|---------|
|  A  |     99    |  98  |    97   |
|  B  |     92    |  91  |    90   |
|  C  |     88    |  87  |    86   |
+-----+-----------+------|---------|
```
请使用 SQL 代码将以上成绩转换为如下格式：
```plain
+-----+-----------+------|
| name|   subject |score |
+-----+-----------+------|
|  A  |  chinese  |  99  |
|  A  |  math     |  98  |
|  A  |  english  |  97  |
|  B  |  chinese  |  92  |
|  B  |  math     |  91  |
|  B  |  english  |  90  |
|  C  |  chinese  |  88  |
|  C  |  math     |  87  |
|  C  |  english  |  86  |
+-----+-----------+------|
```
